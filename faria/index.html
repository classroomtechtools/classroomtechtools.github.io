Teachers in the Driver’s Seat

Teachers just want to get on with the work, right? So how does Faria Group make this possible?

Design Tasks

The heart and soul of learning is spacing our both formative and summative assessments. From low stakes to high stakes, your decide:

  • Low-stakes assessments that are comment only
  • High-stakes assessments that match grading criteria

At Their own Pace

Leadership, or maybe just because teachers' creative juices are flowing, probably need to develop “new” units ahead of time, while also having to teach the “old” curriculum. ManageBac lets you:

  • Switch tasks between Active and Draft mode.
  • Collaborate with colleagues with clear visibility of changes made

Shared between Grades/Classes

Your teachers work in teams face-to-face, and can do so in teams online as well.

  • The tasks can be automatically copied across, with different due dates if necessary.
  • Units which connect to the units can also be shared across and updated automatically

Connected to Units

You can have isolated tasks not connected to any unit, but we all know that building a curriculum means creating links between tasks and the overall big picture.

Curate Content

No matter what curriculum being delivered, ManageBac simplifies the complexity.

Connections

Objectives that connect to Standards, that connect to Skills

There are so many moving pieces in a unit planner, but what remains the same is that ManageBac got your teachers covered:

  • Aims & Objectives
  • Syllabus Content
  • Standards
  • Content, Skills & Concepts

Reflect!

Teachers have a variety of ways to enter their reflections, either per section, or overall in the unit.

You can even carry over reflections from previous years. Of, if you prefer teachers enter them fresh, you can do that too.

Share Out Templates

Increasingly, a school’s digital devices are being used in an educational setting to create files that allow students to modify. Instead of handouts, teachers are creating templated documents.

Dropbox Coursework Submissions

Students can submit their documents to drop box, and if you have Turnitin integrationenabled, it reports the originality check right there.

Templates from Google Docs

Due to the Google Drive integration, teachers can use a Google Doc starting document, and student get a copy of it right from within the ManageBac interface.

Or, if you prefer, it can be accessed through Google Drive directly.

Develop Learning Experiences

Units come with three main tabs:

  • At-a-Glance
  • Planner
  • Stream & Resources
  • Assessment

Stream

Teachers can use their ManageBac classes Stream feature for the following:

  • Add Resource
  • Assessment Task
  • Lesson Experience
  • Online Lesson (with Zoom)

Resources

Each unit contains curated content, and many kinds of resources can be added:

  • Add Files, or Link Files
  • Add Photos
  • Add a Note
  • Add from Google Drive

Presentation

Teachers can display the finished product by going into “Presentation Mode” where all the materials built up is shown, along with tasks.
